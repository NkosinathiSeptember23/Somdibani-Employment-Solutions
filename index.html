About Us

At Somdibani Employment Solutions, we are dedicated to empowering job seekers and businesses. We specialize in revamping CVs, ensuring they highlight your skills and experience in the best light to attract employers. In addition to resume building, we assist in applying for jobs that align with your career goals, we make numerous job applications on your behalf each day. Our team also provides tailored interview coaching, preparing you with potential questions and strategies for making a strong impression.

To access these services, our clients need to pay a subscription fee of R250, which covers a two-months period. This fee also includes transport assistance for any interviews you secure through our platform. At Somdibani, we aim to foster long-term relationships, empower job seekers with the skills they need, and help businesses find the best talent. Whether you are new to the job market or looking for your next career opportunity, we are here to support you every step of the way.
